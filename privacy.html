Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. No Data Collection (데이터 수집 거부)

1SecTools operates on a strict "No Data Saved" principle. All of our tools (including thumbnail downloaders, tax calculators, and text formatting) run entirely locally in your web browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any text, images, or files you input into our tools to any external servers.

2. Local Processing (로컬 브라우저 처리)

Your privacy is mathematically guaranteed because our server infrastructure does not receive your data. Everything happens on your device. Once you close the tab, the session data is permanently gone.

3. Third-Party Services (제3자 서비스)

To keep this site free, we use third-party services like Google AdSense for advertising and Google Analytics to monitor website traffic. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Log Data (로그 데이터)

Like most website operators, our hosting provider may collect standard log data such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamp when you visit our site. This is used strictly for security, abuse prevention, and network diagnostics.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact page.
